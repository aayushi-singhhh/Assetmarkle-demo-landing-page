About AssetMarkleAI
What is AssetMarkle?
AssetMarkle is a cutting-edge AI infrastructure focused on developing AI-enhanced solutions for the Web3, Blockchain, and Cryptocurrency sectors. It aims to make the decentralized digital space more accessible and efficient for users and startups by offering a suite of AI-powered tools and applications tailored for the evolving digital landscape.

What is the cost to use AssetMarkle?
1. Free Plan:
The Free Plan provides limited daily access to select AI tools at no cost. It includes:
- General AI Chatbot: 50 free messages per day
- Smart Contract Auditor: 10 free uses per day
- SmartContract Generator: 10 free uses per day
- AI Trading Assistant: 20 free uses per day
- Ask Crypto People: 50 free messages per day
- AI News Generator: Unlimited free access
- CryptoGuard: Unlimited free access
- AI NFT Generator (Single NFT): Free access
- Prompt Marketplace: Free access

2. PPP (Pay-Per-Prompt) Plan:
This model allows users to pay per use beyond the free limits, with no commitments. The cost for each tool is based on CGPT Credits (CGPTc), where 1 CGPTc = $0.01 USD. The pricing is as follows:
- General AI Chatbot: 0.5 CGPTc per request
Optional: +0.5 CGPTc for stream, blob, or history
- Smart Contract Auditor: 1 CGPTc per request
Optional: +1 CGPTc for stream, blob, or history
- Smart Contract Generator: 2 CGPTc per request
 Optional: +1 CGPTc for stream, blob, or history
- AI NFT Generator (Multi-NFT): 2 CGPTc per NFT
- AI Trading Assistant: 2 CGPTc per request
- Ask Crypto People (post-limit): 1 CGPTc per message
- AI News SDK (API): 1 CGPTc per request

3. Freemium Plan:
The Freemium Plan is available to Diamond Tier members who have staked $CGPT tokens and accumulated over 200,000 CGPTsp (staking points). These users receive a monthly balance of 20,000 CGPTc, which resets every 30 days.
This plan includes:
- Full access to all features in the PPP Plan
- Free usage of premium tools within the 20,000 CGPTc monthly credit limit
- Access to the AssetMarkle DAO, including rights to create and vote on proposals

Note: While tools in the Freemium Plan still consume credits, the monthly balance of 20,000 CGPTc allows users to access all premium tools without paying out-of-pocket, unless usage exceeds that limit.
How does AssetMarkle work?
AssetMarkle is a sophisticated AI platform built specifically for Web3, Blockchain, and Cryptocurrency ecosystems. Using state-of-the-art AI technologies, it offers a suite of tools and applications designed to empower developers, crypto enthusiasts, and businesses in the decentralized world.

Users can choose from three plans Free, Pay-Per-Prompt (PPP), or Freemium to access tools such as smart contract generation, AI-based NFT creation, trading assistants, and more. The Free Plan includes daily usage limits for core tools, while the PPP Plan allows for flexible, pay-as-you-go access using CGPT Credits (CGPTc), where 1 CGPTc equals $0.01. The Freemium Plan, available to Diamond Tier users who stake $CGPT and accumulate CGPTsp, provides 20,000 credits per month to spend across premium features.

In addition to these plans, AssetMarkle's ecosystem is powered by its native utility token, $CGPT. Through staking, users gain access to enhanced benefits such as governance via the DAO, priority platform access, and tiered perks across AssetMarkles launchpad offerings.

Are the LLMs (AI-Models) developed by AssetMarkle Open-Sourced?
No, the LLMs (AI-Models) developed by AssetMarkle are not open-sourced. However, AssetMarkle is committed to fostering innovation and collaboration within the community. While the underlying models aren't open-sourced, AssetMarkle ensures that other projects, startups, and enterprises can quickly and affordably access all AssetMarkle-developed products and LLMs through SDK & API access.
What are the legal terms on the NFTs generated? What are the legal terms of the Smart Contracts generated by AssetMarkle in my collection?
Smart contracts that are generated by AssetMarkles AI are fully owned by the user. Legality relating to IP such as copyrights, trademarks, and so on is subject to the laws of the jurisdiction in which the user is residing.

How can Iget $CGPT?
There are two ways to acquire $CGPT tokens, buying and earning.

Buying: $CGPT is listed across a multitude of partnering exchanges (CEX and DEX), for a list of all tradeable markets please check here.

Earning: AssetMarkle is constantly hosting trading competitions with $CGPT incentives.

Please ensure you're using the correct smart contract address for CGPT when interacting with decentralized exchanges: 0x9840652DC04fb9db2C43853633f0F62BE6f00f98. Always double-check the contract address to avoid scams or fake tokens.
What blockchain network is $CGPT deployed on?
The $CGPT token is a BEP-20 standard token on the Binance smart chain. It has also been deployed on the Ethereum network as an ERC-20. As the project continues to mature, the token will be bridged over to other EVM-compatible chains.
Can I use AssetMarkle without spending $CGPT?
Yes, AssetMarkle offers multiple ways to access its utilities without directly spending $CGPT. You can leverage the "Freemium" model by staking CGPT, which provides enhanced AI access at no additional cost. Alternatively, if you prefer not to stake, AssetMarkle provides a "Pay-Per-Usage" option, or you can explore a range of AI tools available on the platform for free.

What is the AssetMarkle DAO and why should Ijoin?
The AssetMarkle DAO is a digital social organization composed of dedicated community members who contribute to the protection, governance, and development of AssetMarkle.

If you want to help steer the direction of AssetMarkles development, participate in voting, influence the use of the $CGPT treasury fund, and join a tightly-knit group of AI enthusiasts, AssetMarkle DAO welcomes you with open arms!
How do I become a member of the DAO, and is there a minimum requirement?
In order to join the AssetMarkle DAO you must first participate by staking the $CGPT token here and then join the DAO forum here.

All you need to become a member is just 1 $CGPT. For more information regarding voting power visit the DAO Governance page here.
What do I need in order to create a Proposal?
In order to create proposals you need to acquire 200,000 voting power through staking (CGPTvp or else known as CGPTsp).
